# BYLAWS OF RIVERWALK CONDOMINIUM ASSOCIATION, INC.

<Callout type="info" title="About These Bylaws">
  These bylaws outline the governance structure and rules for the Riverwalk Condominium Association, Inc. This document is essential for owners, residents, and board members.
</Callout>

## ARTICLE I - NATURE OF BYLAWS

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1.01 Purpose">
    These Bylaws are intended to govern the administration of Riverwalk Condominium Association, Inc. (the "Association"), a non-profit corporation organized under Title 15A of the New Jersey Statutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="1.02 Definitions">
    Unless the context clearly indicates otherwise, all definitions set forth in the Master Deed or in N.J.S.A. 46:8B-3 are incorporated herein by reference.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="1.03 Fiscal Year">
    The fiscal year of the corporation shall be determined by the Board of Directors.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="1.04 Principal Office">
    The principal office of the corporation is located at **32 West Front Street, Red Bank, New Jersey 07701**, or such other location as may be determined by the Board of Directors.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE II - MEMBERSHIP AND VOTING RIGHTS

<Callout type="warning" title="Membership Eligibility">
  Membership is automatically granted to property owners. However, tenants or lessees may only become associate members without voting rights.
</Callout>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="2.01 Members">
    Every person, firm, association, corporation, or other legal entity who is a record owner or co-owner of a Unit is a Member of the Association.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2.02 Member in Good Standing">
    A Member must be current on all assessments, penalties, and charges to maintain good standing and voting rights.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2.06 Suspension of Rights">
    Membership and voting rights may be suspended for failure to pay assessments or violations of Association rules.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE III - MEETINGS OF OWNERS

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="3.01 Place of Meetings">
    All meetings of Owners shall be held at the Condominium or a location designated by the Board.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3.05 Quorum">
    A quorum shall consist of Members holding at least **25% of the authorized votes**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE IV - BOARD OF DIRECTORS

<Callout type="tip" title="Board Responsibilities">
  The Board of Directors is responsible for managing the Association, enforcing rules, and overseeing financial matters.
</Callout>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="4.01 Qualifications">
    Directors must be Members in Good Standing. Corporations and joint owners may designate a representative.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4.03 Removal of Directors">
    Directors may be removed **with cause** by a vote of the Owners at a duly held meeting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE VII - FISCAL MANAGEMENT

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="7.01 Budget and Assessments">
    The Board shall prepare an **annual budget** and levy assessments for maintaining the Condominium.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="7.03 Reserves">
    The Board shall maintain reserve funds for **future repairs and improvements**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE IX - COMPENSATION AND INDEMNIFICATION

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="9.02 Indemnification">
    The Association shall indemnify Directors and officers against losses incurred in their official capacity, except in cases of **gross negligence or willful misconduct**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE X - ENFORCEMENT

<Callout type="danger" title="Rule Enforcement">
  The Board has the authority to enforce rules through fines, legal action, and other measures to maintain order within the community.
</Callout>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="10.02 Fines">
    Fines may be imposed for violations, with prior notice and an opportunity for a hearing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## ARTICLE XII - MISCELLANEOUS

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="12.01 Conflict Resolution">
    If any provision of these Bylaws conflicts with the Master Deed, the **Master Deed shall control**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

***

These Bylaws are adopted and shall govern the Association in accordance with applicable laws.
